Equations are short ways of representing complex ideas. in chemistry, equations tell us how many atoms of each element are needed to make a new molecule. For example, the chemical equation for water is H₂O. this equation tells us that a molecule of water is made from two hydrogen atoms attached to one oxygen atom. The chemical equation for carbon dioxide is CO2.
